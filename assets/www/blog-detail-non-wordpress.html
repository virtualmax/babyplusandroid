Premier League Weekend 1st September 2012

19 Sep 2012 (Football Accumulators)

After the goals, goals and more goals of the opening weekend of the premier league we were given another goal fest during the week when Chelsea played Reading at Stamford Bridge for their second game of the season, and at 2-2 it could of gone either way! Looking forward to this weekend sees some good ties and below is some information on them.

Swansea Vs West Ham

After watching Swansea tear QPR apart last weekend you’d think I’d go with a Swansea win here, but during preseason they conceded a fair amount of goals and West Ham looked good going forward. I’d look at a score draw bet here, with more than one goal each.

Aston Villa Vs Everton

If Everton play the same as they played against Manchester United monday night then there is no reason why they can’t push for a top 7 finish this season, based the Monday night performance alone of Everton, and the defending that went with it I’d go for an Everton win at 2-0.

Manchester United Vs Fulham

Fulham didn’t look like they missed Clint Dempsy at the weekend, but will they be able to do the same against a Manchester United team looking for a reaction after the defeat at Everton, Probably Not! I’d expect United to win this by a couple of goals, So probably a Manchester United win, 3-0. I’d look at Robin Van Persie to score in the 90 minutes as I would image he has to start the game with it being the first home game.

Chelsea Vs Newcastle

Watching Chelsea during the week against Reading, they do look like they can be broken down. Newcastle are looking as good as they did last season so I can see them pushing Chelsea in this game. There will be goals, I’m going for a Newcastle win, 2-1.
